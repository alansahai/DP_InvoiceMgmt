E. Q&A Cheat Sheet

How is this different from generic OCR?
We combine extraction with trust scoring, compliance checks, duplicate controls, and workflow routing.
How do you prevent hallucinated AI outputs?
Structured JSON schema, confidence thresholds, validation rules, and explicit fail states.
What if vendors use inconsistent formats?
GenAI handles format variability; rules normalize and flag inconsistencies.
How do you detect duplicates reliably?
SHA-256 document hash + business-key checks (vendor/date/amount, invoice number where available).
Can this scale beyond demo?
Yes—modular pipeline, stateless processing, DB-backed audit trail, and webhook integration path.
How secure is it?
Env-based secrets, no hardcoded credentials, and controlled data flow/storage.
What happens on extraction failure?
No silent fallback; explicit FAILED status and review/ retry path.
How do finance teams trust decisions?
Trust Panel exposes confidence, scores, reason codes, and routing rationale.
What ROI can a company expect?
Major cycle-time reduction, lower manual errors, and fewer duplicate payouts.

Pilot with one AP inbox, collect precision/latency metrics, then expand connectors and policy packs.
